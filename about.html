ABOUT LEO

La Martiniere Extra-Curricular Organization (LEO) is an umbrella group made up of representatives of the different Co-Curricular Activity (CCA) clubs that function in the College. The members of LEO see themselves in the role of leadership as lions. They have chosen, for their inspiration, the ubiquitous lion emblem that embellishes the monument Constantia, which is also the name of this event. The apex of the monument Constantia bears a unique crown, which contributes to the image of the crowned lion that is the mascot for Constantia.


CONSTANTIA 2023
